Mint tea

Description

With just two ingredients you will be able to enjoy one of the most cozy and fresh drinks that you can have in these lazy days.

Ingredients:

  • 250 ml of wather
  • As much as you want of freash mint leaves
  • Sugar (optional)

How to make

  1. Boil: Boil the wather.
  2. Pick the mint: Add the freash mint leaves to a cute mug.
  3. Steep:
    4. Fill up the mug with the boiled wather. Allow to stand for 5 minutes. Enjoy!
