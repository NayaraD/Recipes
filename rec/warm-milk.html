Warm milk

Description

This is the perfect recipe for the bedtime! It will warm you up and make you have a good night of sleep.

Ingredients

  • Milk

How to make

  1. Choose your favorite night mug.
  2. To boil the milk, you can either use a kettle or a microwave
  3. Put the boiled milk in the mug, wait for it to cool down a little and enjoy!
