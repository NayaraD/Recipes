Peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Description

Nothing more cozy than this classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sandwich bread
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons of your favorite flavor of jelly

How to make

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  2. Spred the jelly on the other side
  3. Put the two pieces of bread togheter to form a sandwich
  4. Cut in half forming two triangles. Enjoy!
